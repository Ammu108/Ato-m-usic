Services That We Are Providing

Welcome to Ato-m-usic Studio, where your musical vision comes to life. We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to meet the needs of musicians, bands, and audio professionals. Below is an overview of our services along with average pricing to help you plan your next project.

Recording Services

Capture the essence of your music with our state-of-the-art recording facilities. Our experienced engineers ensure the highest quality sound for your tracks.
Hourly Recording Session: $75/hour
Full-Day Recording Session (8 hours): $550
Half-Day Recording Session (4 hours): $300

Mixing Services

Bring out the best in your recordings with our professional mixing services. We tailor the mix to your artistic vision, ensuring clarity and balance.

Album Production (up to 12 songs): $5,000

Rehearsal Space

Need a place to practice? Our comfortable and well-equipped rehearsal rooms are perfect for solo musicians and bands alike.

Full Day Rehearsal: $100/hour
Monthly Rehearsal Package (20 hours): $500

Instrument Rentals

We offer a range of high-quality instruments and gear available for rent during your sessions.
Guitar/Bass: $25/day
Keyboard: $40/day
Drum Kit: $50/day

Custom Services

Looking for something specific? We offer tailored services to meet unique needs, including custom arrangements, remixing, and more. Contact us for a personalized quote.
